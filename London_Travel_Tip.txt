Travel Tip: Don't miss the chance to explore the historic landmarks of London like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and Big Ben. If you're a nature lover, make sure to spare some time for a relaxing walk in Hyde Park or Regent's Park. For an authentic London experience, try the local delicacies like fish and chips or an English breakfast. Lastly, traveling by the iconic double-decker buses is a great way to see the city while staying on budget.
